Check out if your Birthdate is Palidrome. Github Repo

A palindrome is a word/number which reads the same backward as forward

For eg: madam, 12321 reads same when read backward & forward




More

Enter your birthdate and we will tell you if your birthdate is a palindrome or not.

This app checks your birthdate in 3 formats yyyy-mm-dd, dd-mm-yyyy, mm-dd-yyyy

e.g. if your birthdate is 18 Nov 1997, then app will check for 1997-11-18, 18-11-1997, 11-18-1997
